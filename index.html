Why Twelve?



We created Twelve on the age-old principle of collaboration. We recognise that the vast majority of millennial investors have neither the time nor absolute resources to properly select, do due diligence and take advantage of investment opportunities in Nigeria.
By ensuring that every investment on our platform is introduced by a verified investment partner, Twelve enables investors to collaborate and benefit from premium investment opportunities which increases the likelihood of achieving their investment goals.

Collaborate. Save time. Achieve your goals.



How It Works



You tell us your goals

What are you investing for? Are you about to start an investment club? Or have you started one with your tribe already? Do you want to find a project to co-invest in?

You create an account

Once we know what your goals are, we will show you how to create an account with us easily to get started on your investment journey. No matter your goals are, Twelve can help.

We share our opportunities

We’ll share credible investment opportunities from our investment partners with you. We work hard to ensure our partners provide diversified investment opportunities for you.

You monitor your investment

This is where it gets interesting. You can log in to view your investments in one place on our platform and from anywhere in the world. Sounds like fun, right?
