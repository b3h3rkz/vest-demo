Why Twelve?

We created Twelve on the principle of collaboration. We recognise that the vast majority of millennial investors have neither the time nor absolute resources to properly select, do due diligence and invest in investment opportunities in Nigeria. By ensuring that every investment on our platform is introduced by a verified investment partner, Twelve enables investors to collaborate and benefit from premium investment opportunities which increases the likelihood of achieving their investment goals.
